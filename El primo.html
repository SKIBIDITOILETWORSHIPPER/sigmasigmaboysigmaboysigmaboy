History

Formation and Pre-Debut Name and Meaning: The group’s name, "LE SSERAFIM," is an anagram of "I'M FEARLESS," signifying self-confidence and resilience. Initial Concept: LE SSERAFIM was envisioned as a group that breaks conventional standards in the K-pop industry, emphasizing individuality and boldness. Debut Lineup LE SSERAFIM initially consisted of six members: Kim Chaewon: A former member of IZ*ONE and the group leader. Sakura Miyawaki: A popular Japanese idol and also a former member of IZ*ONE. Huh Yunjin: A Korean-American trainee known from the show "Produce 48." Kazuha Nakamura: A Japanese member with a professional ballet background. Hong Eunchae: The youngest member (maknae) of the group. Kim Garam: Initially part of the lineup but departed shortly after debut due to controversy. Debut and Early Career Debut Album: LE SSERAFIM debuted on May 2, 2022, with their mini-album FEARLESS. The title track, also named "FEARLESS," showcases the group’s bold concept and confident energy. Initial Success: The album gained commercial success, making an impact on various Korean and international music charts. Changes in Lineup Kim Garam’s Departure: Shortly after debut, Kim Garam faced allegations of school bullying, which led to a hiatus and eventual departure from the group. LE SSERAFIM continued as a five-member group. Further Releases and Achievements ANTIFRAGILE (October 17, 2022): Their second mini-album, showcasing themes of growth and resilience. The title track "ANTIFRAGILE" became a massive hit, solidifying their reputation in the K-pop industry. UNFORGIVEN (May 1, 2023): This album incorporated Western and cinematic influences, and its title track, featuring Nile Rodgers, demonstrated the group’s artistic evolution. Notable Achievements LE SSERAFIM became one of the fastest fourth-generation K-pop groups to gain international recognition, frequently topping charts on platforms like Spotify and Billboard. The group is known for their high-energy performances, engaging music videos, and versatile discography. Brand and Influence The members have individually and collectively represented major brands like Louis Vuitton, YSL, and others, reflecting their impact on both music and fashion. Their bold, empowering themes resonate with fans globally, fostering a dedicated fanbase known as FEARNOT.
