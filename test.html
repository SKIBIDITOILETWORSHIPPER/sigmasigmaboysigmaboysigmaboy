Members of Le Sserafim.

Sakura Miyawaki

Introduction Date: April 4, 2022 Background: Born on March 19, 1998, in Kagoshima, Japan, Sakura is a Japanese singer and actress. She was a member of the Japanese idol group HKT48 and gained international fame as a member of the project girl group IZONE from 2018 to 2021. After IZONE's disbandment, she returned to Japan before joining LE SSERAFIM

Kim Garam

Introduction Date: April 5, 2022 Background: Born on November 16, 2005, in Sangju, South Korea, Garam was introduced as a member of LE SSERAFIM but departed from the group on July 20, 2022, following controversies.

Hong Eunchae

Introduction Date: April 6, 2022 Background: Born on November 10, 2006, in South Korea, Eunchae is the youngest member (maknae) of LE SSERAFIM. She trained under Source Music before debuting with the group.

Kim Chaewon

Introduction Date: April 7, 2022 Background: Born on August 1, 2000, in Seoul, South Korea, Chaewon is the leader of LE SSERAFIM. She was previously a member of IZONE alongside Sakura. After IZONE's disbandment, she signed with Source Music and re-debuted with LE SSERAFIM.

Kazuha Nakamura

Introduction Date: April 8, 2022 Background: Born on August 9, 2003, in Kochi, Japan, Kazuha is a Japanese member of LE SSERAFIM. She trained as a ballerina in the Netherlands before transitioning to K-pop and joining the group.

Huh Yunjin

Introduction Date: April 9, 2022 Background: Born on October 8, 2001, in Seoul, South Korea, Yunjin moved to the United States at a young age. She participated in the reality show "Produce 48" in 2018 but did not make it to the final lineup of IZ*ONE. She later joined Source Music and debuted with LE SSERAFIM.
